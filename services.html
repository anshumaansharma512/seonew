Good To Know

Services FAQ

Do you offer walk-in services or is booking required?

Walk-ins are welcome for everyday services like threading, waxing and haircuts. For facials, hair colour, keratin treatments and makeup, we recommend messaging us on WhatsApp first to check availability.

Which brands do you use for facials and skincare?

We use premium brands including O3+, Lotus, Shahnaz and Aroma Magic across our facial and skincare treatments.

Can prices vary based on hair length or skin condition?

Yes — services like hair spa, hair colour and waxing are priced by hair length, and some skin treatments may be adjusted after a consultation. Message us on WhatsApp for an exact quote.
