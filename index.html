Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting Ambika Makeover Salon

Where is Ambika Makeover Salon located?

We are located on the 1st Floor, Ramanuj Market, NH139, above Union Bank, Naubatpur, Patna, Bihar 801109.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Walk-ins are welcome for regular services, but we recommend booking bridal, HD and airbrush makeup packages in advance on WhatsApp to secure your date.

What are your salon timings?

Ambika Makeover Salon is open daily from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

How much does bridal makeup cost at Ambika Makeover Salon?

Bridal makeup starts at ₹8,000, HD makeup at ₹10,000, and airbrush makeup at ₹15,000. Pre-bridal packages are available on request via WhatsApp.
