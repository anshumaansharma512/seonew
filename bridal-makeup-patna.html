Bridal Makeup FAQ

How much does bridal makeup cost in Patna at Ambika Makeover Salon?

Bridal makeup starts at ₹8,000, HD bridal makeup at ₹10,000, and airbrush bridal makeup at ₹15,000. Final pricing depends on the package and any add-ons you choose.

How far in advance should I book my bridal makeup?

We recommend booking your bridal makeup consultation on WhatsApp at least 3-4 weeks before your wedding date to secure your preferred date and time, especially during wedding season.

Do you offer pre-bridal packages?

Yes, we offer pre-bridal packages covering skincare and beauty rituals in the weeks leading up to your wedding. Contact us on WhatsApp for custom pricing based on your needs and timeline.

What's the difference between HD makeup and airbrush makeup?

HD makeup gives a high-definition, camera-ready finish that looks flawless in photos and videos. Airbrush makeup is applied with a fine mist for an ultra-lightweight, long-lasting, breathable finish — ideal for long wedding days.
